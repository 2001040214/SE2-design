A03:2025 — Software Supply Chain Failures

OWASP Top 10 (2025) • Lesson page

What you’ll learn

  • Understand supply chain risk across code, dependencies, build, and deployment.
  • Recognize attacks: dependency confusion, typosquatting, poisoned updates.
  • Identify weak points in CI/CD (tokens, build runners, artifacts, registries).
  • Use SBOM concepts and dependency hygiene (pinning, lockfiles, reviews).
  • Apply signing & provenance (artifact integrity, trusted publishing).
  • Design guardrails: least privilege pipelines, protected branches, monitoring.
