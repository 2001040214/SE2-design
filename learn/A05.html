A05:2025 — Injection

OWASP Top 10 (2025) • Lesson page

What you’ll learn

  • Understand injection as untrusted input influencing an interpreter or query.
  • Recognize key types: SQL/NoSQL, OS command, LDAP, template injection.
  • Map injection impact: data theft, RCE, privilege escalation, business abuse.
  • Prevent injection with parameterization, safe APIs, and strict allow-lists.
  • Test for injection using payload strategy and evidence-based reporting.
  • Reduce blast radius: least privilege DB users, sandboxing, monitoring.
