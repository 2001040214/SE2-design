Question 1
Credential stuffing is best described as…
  • A. Guessing random passwords slowly
  • B. Trying leaked username/password pairs across many sites
  • C. Injecting SQL into a login form
  • D. Changing URLs to access admin pages
Hint: Reuse of leaked creds.
Show answer
Correct: B. Trying leaked username/password pairs across many sites
Explanation: Reuse of leaked creds.
Question 2

  • A. Longer usernames
  • B. Rate limiting + lockout policies + MFA
  • C. Disabling TLS

Show answer
Correct: B. Rate limiting + lockout policies + MFA
