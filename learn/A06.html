A06:2025 — Insecure Design

OWASP Top 10 (2025) • Lesson page

What you’ll learn

  • Understand “insecure design” vs implementation bugs.
  • Identify missing controls: rate limits, workflows, abuse cases, trust boundaries.
  • Use threat modeling to find design-level flaws early.
  • Design secure workflows: validation, authorization, anti-automation, and safe defaults.
  • Apply secure patterns: defense in depth, least privilege, and secure-by-design reviews.
  • Write security requirements and test cases for design risks.
