A04:2025 — Cryptographic Failures

OWASP Top 10 (2025) • Lesson page

What you’ll learn

  • Know when encryption is required (in transit and at rest) and what to protect.
  • Recognize weak crypto: outdated algorithms, bad modes, poor randomness.
  • Understand TLS basics and common mistakes (protocols, ciphers, validation).
  • Handle keys safely: generation, storage, rotation, and access controls.
  • Test crypto controls: TLS checks, data exposure mapping, key usage review.
  • Apply secure patterns: modern algorithms, libraries, and safe defaults.
